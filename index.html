Who am I?

Born in Russia, Saint-Petersburg, my drive and ambition have led me on to the road where I find myself now. In reading of the tremendous opportunities for growth and development that a Western education is able to afford, at the age of 14, my restless desire to learn took me to the United Kingdom. In pursuit of a career that would one day, secure my entry in to the pinnacle of the commercial sector, the world-renowned position of the United Kingdom, specifically, London itself, as a paradigm and hub of global business and learning, bound my desire to grow in such an environment of matchless stature.

The fire that was there ignited within me has continued to spur my urge for learning business operations and motivated me throughout my educational path. Undertaking a Bachelor’s degree in Business Management at the University of Surrey and a Master’s degree in Management at Queen Mary University, London, my interests in the sales and marketing spheres has been greatly enhanced, underlining my understanding of how a successful career remains contingent upon certain aspects of business operation that remain enduring fundamentals. The unfading yearning that underpins my drive to succeed has brought me to where I am now, and informs my unwavering desire to grow in knowledge and experience.
